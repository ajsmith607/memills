VII. GENERATION. 205 

Lee, June 14, 1838, who was b. in Leverett, Mh. 15, 1816, the son of Charles Lee, and nephew of the late Hon. Gideon Lee, formerly Mayor of N. York. He was a merchant in New York city, but now in Syracuse, N. Y.
